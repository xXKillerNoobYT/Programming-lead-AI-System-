**Deferred to #148** (2 NITs outside #146's AC):

**Why the height bug was fixed inline, not deferred**: NEEDS REVISION overrides the close-the-review-loop deferral rule when the finding is non-cosmetic. Scroll containment breaking on short viewports is a real correctness issue, not a NIT.
